How It Works

Simple, efficient operation for improved dental procedures

1

Connect & Prepare

Attach the disposable tip to the main device body and connect to your practice's suction system or use the built-in suction capability.

2

Position & Activate

Place the ergonomic device in the patient's mouth at the optimal position and activate suction with the touch-sensitive control panel.

3

Adjust & Monitor

Fine-tune suction power as needed during the procedure. The transparent collection chamber allows for easy monitoring of fluid levels.

4

Clean & Sterilize

After use, dispose of the single-use tip and clean the device according to our simple sterilization protocol for the next patient.
