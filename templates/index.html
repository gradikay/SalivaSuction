How It Works

Simple, efficient operation for effective nasal care

1

Prepare Device

Attach the appropriate size silicone tip for comfort and optimal suction. Our color-coded tips make it easy to select the right size.

2

Select Power Level

Choose from three power settings using the intuitive control panel: Gentle (for infants), Medium (for toddlers), and Strong (for older children and adults).

3

Clear Nasal Passages

Gently place the tip at the nostril entrance and activate suction. The ergonomic design makes it easy to maintain proper positioning.

4

Clean & Store

Detach the collection container, rinse components under warm water, and allow to air dry before reassembling for next use.
